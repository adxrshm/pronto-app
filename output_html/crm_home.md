# Customer Relationship Management

The Customer Relationship Management (CRM) module is a business tool designed to optimise profitability, revenue and customer satisfaction by organising the business around customer segments, fostering customer-satisfying behaviours and linking processes from prospects through to customers. CRM is a customer intelligence tool designed to assist in such activities as:

* sales force automation
* customer service and support
* marketing opportunities

The Customer Relationship Management module provides a complete picture of a customer throughout the entire lifecycle. It leverages existing information through integration with all other Pronto Xi modules which makes it a truly integrated Enterprise Resource System. These modules are:

* Accounts Receivable
* Quality Management System
* Inventory
* Service
* Sales Orders
