# Accounts Payable

Pronto Xi Accounts Payable provides all the features you need to control your supplier accounts.

These features include:

* automatic cheque generation
* purchase history
* [foreign currency](javascript:void(0);)A currency, other than the (default) local currency, that is set up for transactional or reporting purposes. payments
* Withholding Tax payments
* processing of periodic payments
* production of remittance notes

Accounts Payable is fully integrated with General Ledger and Purchasing.

Activities on a purchase order raised against a supplier can be traced through the supplier’s account in Accounts Payable. These in turn automatically update affected accounts in the General Ledger.

Project can be added to extend Accounts Payable to a full project system.
